Services

Whole Home Inspections

  • A visual, non-invasive inspection of the homes foundation, plumbing, electrical, heating and cooling systems, built in appliances, exterior, interior, and safety. This type of inspections is most common for those purchasing or selling a home.

4 point inspections

  • A limited visual, non-invasive inspection of the homes roof, electrical, plumbing, and HVAC systems. This inspection is typically required by insurance companies.

11 month warranty inspections

  • A visual, non-invasive inspection of the homes foundation, plumbing, electrical, heating and cooling systems, built in appliances, exterior, interior, and safety. This type of inspection is for those that are nearing the end of their builders warranty.

Wind Mitigation

  • A visual, non-invasive inspection of a homes resistance and protection features against severe storms or hurricanes. Qualifying homes may receive credits from their insurance companies and may save money.
